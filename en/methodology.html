Methodology And Data Standards

Korea Stock Radar is not a stock-picking or price-prediction service. This page explains how links, themes, and automated news modules are selected and maintained.

Data And Link Sources

  • Finance links point to external public pages such as Naver Finance and Daum Finance.
  • News links point to Naver News Search and Google News Search results.
  • Automated news modules use cached search results. Article details should be checked at the original publisher.
  • U.S. listing and filing links are provided for source checking through external sites such as SEC and Google News.

Theme Selection

Theme pages are created around high-interest search keywords and repeated market-news events, including HBM, defense, shipbuilding, nuclear power, robotics, SpaceX, and Starlink. Inclusion in a theme does not mean direct exposure, verified benefit, or investment attractiveness.

Update Process

Some theme news modules are refreshed by automation scripts. Automated results are research shortcuts and may be delayed or affected by external search quality. Before making any financial decision, readers should re-check original articles, filings, earnings releases, exchange data, and brokerage data.

Corrections Policy

If you find an error, outdated information, or wording that could overstate a direct beneficiary relationship, please report it through GitHub Issues. Verifiable errors are corrected, and theme language is kept conservative when a relationship is only keyword-based.

Investment-Information Limits

Korea Stock Radar is not a licensed financial adviser and does not provide investment advice, discretionary management, buy or sell recommendations, or performance guarantees. All information is for research and navigation only.
