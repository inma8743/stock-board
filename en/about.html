About Korea Stock Radar

Korea Stock Radar does not provide investment advice, discretionary investment management, buy or sell recommendations, or performance promises. All information is provided for research and navigation only.

Purpose

Korea Stock Radar is a free information hub for high-interest Korean stock market keywords such as KOSPI, KOSDAQ, Samsung Electronics, SK Hynix, SpaceX, Starlink, HBM, defense, and other market themes. It helps readers find news shortcuts, finance pages, theme checklists, and related research pages in one place.

Editorial Policy

  • We do not tell readers to buy, sell, or hold any security.
  • Theme keywords are separated from verified contracts, filings, revenue exposure, and earnings impact.
  • Global events are not presented as automatic direct benefits for Korean companies.
  • External news and finance links are provided as starting points for reader research.
  • Automatically collected news links should be checked at the original source before making any decision.
