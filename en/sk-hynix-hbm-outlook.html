HBM Outlook Checklist

  • Qualification stage: separate sampling, validation, customer qualification, mass production, shipment, and revenue recognition.
  • Generation transition: compare HBM3E momentum with HBM4 timing, performance, power efficiency, package height, and yield risk.
  • Customer concentration: HBM demand can depend on a narrow set of AI accelerator customers and their launch schedules.
  • Capacity and capex: demand-backed investment can support growth, but overcapacity or customer delays can pressure pricing and margins.
  • Memory cycle: check whether DRAM and NAND pricing confirm the story or whether HBM is masking weakness elsewhere.
