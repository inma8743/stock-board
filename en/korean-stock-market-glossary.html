Korean Stock Market Glossary

Plain-English definitions for Korean market terms overseas readers see when tracking KOSPI, KOSDAQ, Samsung Electronics, SK Hynix, HBM, and theme stocks.

Important: Glossary entries are educational shortcuts, not investment advice. Terms can help you understand news faster, but they do not tell you whether to buy, sell, or hold any security.

KOSPI

Main boardLarge caps

The main Korean stock market board. Overseas readers often use KOSPI headlines to understand broad Korean equity sentiment and large-cap moves.

KOSDAQ

Growth stocksThemes

A Korean market often associated with growth companies, technology, biotech, and small or mid-cap theme trading. Volatility can be higher than large-cap market moves.

KRX

ExchangeMarket data

Korea Exchange. KRX Market Data is a useful starting point for official market statistics, trading value, index data, and listed securities checks.

Foreign Flows

Net buyingNet selling

Net buying or selling by overseas investors. Foreign flows can affect sentiment in large names, but they should be read with earnings, rates, currency, and valuation context.

HBM

AI chipsMemory

High Bandwidth Memory, a memory technology tied to AI accelerators. In Korea, HBM headlines often affect Samsung Electronics, SK Hynix, and semiconductor supply-chain themes.

Theme Stocks

NarrativeRisk

Stocks grouped around a market narrative such as SpaceX, Starlink, defense, robotics, nuclear power, or AI chips. A theme label does not prove revenue exposure.

Disclosure

FilingSource check

Official company announcements and filings. When a news headline claims a contract, investment, or earnings impact, disclosures are one of the first places to verify it.

Naver Finance / Daum Finance

Korean sourcesQuotes

Popular Korean finance portals. They are useful for local quotes, news links, discussion context, and Korean-language market navigation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KOSPI?

KOSPI is the main board often used to track large Korean listed companies and broad Korean equity-market sentiment.

What is KOSDAQ?

KOSDAQ is a Korean market often associated with growth companies, technology, biotech, and theme-driven small and mid-cap stocks.

What are foreign investor flows?

Foreign investor flows describe net buying or selling by overseas investors. They are useful market context, but they are not a standalone investment signal.
